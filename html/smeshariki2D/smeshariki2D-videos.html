The Bench

A Prince For Nyusha

Nyusha reads a book and learns that in the old days princes and knights performed incredible feats in honor of their beautiful ladies. The pig also wants to find such a prince. Maybe it will be one of her friends? But no one is in a hurry to perform a feat for Nyusha: everyone is busy with their own affairs...
