The Bench

Krosh has made a bench and painted it pink. He still has some paint left, and he offers to paint something to Yezhik, but the latter remembers that Barash will probably have something to paint. However, Barash has lost his inspiration. Suddenly it comes, and the poet begins to compose, not noticing how he climbed a tree...

A Prince For Nyusha

Nyusha reads a book and learns that in the old days princes and knights performed incredible feats in honor of their beautiful ladies. The pig also wants to find such a prince. Maybe it will be one of her friends? But no one is in a hurry to perform a feat for Nyusha: everyone is busy with their own affairs...

The Plywood Sun

Everyone's mood changes in the fall. The only thing that could cure this 'disease' is the sun. The question is how can a person's mood get better if there is no sun?

Old Year, New Year

Krosh has tons of things to do that he didn't finish! How will he finish it on December 31? Will he finish the list in time for the New Year?

Snoring Energy (Pin's Scientific Discovery)

Eureka! Pin discovered a new scientific fact: snoring generates wind, wind makes propellers move, and light is shining! But how did Pin discover it? And how does a lightbulb that Krosh and Yezhik found fit in?

The Iron Nanny

What happens when you don't want to do everything yourself? What happens if you want others to do it for you? The answer: create a robot nanny. But things go too far when Krosh and Yezhik discover their robot nanny...

A Gift Of Fate

Fate is kind to most. To Krosh and Yezhik, fate is not kind. To Barash, however, fate is so kind, that it starts giving him gifts...

Who's First?

Who's first - Nyusha or Barash? And why is he/she the first?

Uncultured

There are a lot of weeds in Kopatych's garden - so many that the whole yard is doomed to turn into a real jungle. Fortunately, Krosh and Yezhik, who are nearby, agree to help the bear get rid of the "uncultured plants". And - one, two, they got it!...
